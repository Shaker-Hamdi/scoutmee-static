About Us

WE ARE PASSIONATE ABOUT MUSIC WE COME FROM DIFFERENT COUNTRIES AND WALKS OF LIFE WE ARE MUSICIANS

WE ARE A TEAM OF PEOPLE WHO HAVE DECIDED TO MAKE A CHANGE AND FOCUS 100% ON BUILDING SOMETHING TO HELP OUR COMMUNITY THAT HAS BEEN LOOKING FOR INNOVATION AND DRIVE FOR A LONG TIME

AS MUSICIANS OURSELVES, WE KNOW WELL HOW HARD IT IS TO FIND AN AUDIENCE TO ENGAGE.

INTERACTIONS BETWEEN MUSICIANS AND THE COMMUNITY MAINLY RELY ON WORDOFMOUTH OR UNSUITABLE PLATFORMS, CAUSING STRUGGLE AND OFTEN MISSING SOME GREAT TALENTS WHO ARE SIMPLY NOT VISIBILE YET.

WE WANT EVERY MUSICIAN TO HAVE THE CHANCE TO GET DISCOVERED, WE WANT MUSIC LOVERS TO SCOUT LOCAL TALENTS AND HAVE THE POSSIBILITY TO HIRE THEM FOR LIVE PERFORMANCES AND MUSIC LESSONS.
